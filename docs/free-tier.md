# Free Tier Rules

The free tier is shared across web, mobile, and WordPress.

Rules:
- One company
- Limited invoices per month
- Limited customers
- Limited invoice items per invoice
- One template
- No recurring invoices
- No advanced reports
- No team access
- KYC approval is not required to use the free tier
- Paid plans require submitted KYC/profile documents
- Admin can review and approve/reject submitted documents for both free-tier and paid-tier users
- Signup and login require mobile OTP verification for account security
